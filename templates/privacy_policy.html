Naginawala Privacy Policy

At Naginawala, your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Your Consent

By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our site. Continued use of the site means you accept any changes we may make to this policy.

What Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect technical details such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and how you interact with our site. This helps us improve your experience and keep our site secure.
  • Order Information: When you make a purchase, we collect your name, address, contact details, and payment information to process your order and provide customer support.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and gather analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your orders and deliver products to you.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, updates, or offers (you can opt out of marketing emails at any time).
  • To improve our products, services, and website functionality.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or misuse of our services.
  • For analytics, research, and to personalize your experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

Sharing Your Information

  • We may share your data with trusted third-party partners who help us operate our website, process payments, or deliver orders. These partners are required to keep your data confidential and secure.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with legal proceedings.
  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Newsletter & Communications

  • If you subscribe to our newsletter, you’ll receive updates about new products and offers. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • We use a double opt-in process to confirm your subscription and protect your email address.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
  • European residents have additional rights under GDPR.
  • We retain your order information as long as needed to fulfill your order or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for users over 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Do Not Track

Our website does not respond to “Do Not Track” signals from browsers.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, so please review it regularly.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at support@naginawala.com.
