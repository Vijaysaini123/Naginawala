Naginawala Shipping Policy

At Naginawala, we value your trust and are dedicated to delivering your precious items with the utmost care, security, and transparency. Here’s how we ensure your order reaches you smoothly:

Safe & Secure Delivery

  • Careful Packaging: Every Naginawala order is thoughtfully packed to protect your gemstones, crystals, and spiritual items during transit.
  • Fully Insured: Your shipment is insured until it arrives safely at your doorstep.
  • Trusted Couriers: We partner with reliable courier services to guarantee timely and secure delivery.

Delivery Timelines

  • Within India: Expect your order within 7–15 days from the date of dispatch.
  • International Orders: Delivery usually takes 10–20 days, but may extend to 20–30 days depending on customs and location.
  • Global Reach: We deliver to over 150 countries worldwide.
  • Delays: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs, location, or unforeseen events. We keep you updated with tracking information.

Shipping Charges

  • Transparent Pricing: Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order.
  • Free Shipping: Available for qualifying orders; please check our website or contact support@naginawala.com for current offers.

Address & Delivery

  • Accurate Details: Please provide a complete and correct delivery address at checkout. We deliver only to residential or business addresses, not to PO Boxes, malls, or hotels.
  • Address Changes: If you need to update your address, contact our support team as soon as possible.
  • Customer Responsibility: Naginawala is not responsible for delays or issues caused by incorrect or incomplete address details.

Order Processing & Tracking

  • Quick Dispatch: Orders are processed and shipped within 4 business days of confirmation.
  • Custom Orders: Personalized or custom items may take longer; we’ll keep you informed.
  • Tracking: You’ll receive a tracking link by email once your order is dispatched, along with regular updates via email or SMS.

At Delivery

  • Signature & ID: For high-value orders, we may require a signature and ID verification upon delivery.
  • Missed Deliveries: Our courier will attempt delivery up to three times. If unsuccessful, the package will be returned to us. For rescheduling, contact the courier or our support team promptly.

Handling Delays

  • Force Majeure: Delays due to events beyond our control (natural disasters, strikes, public holidays, etc.) may occur. We’ll keep you informed and provide revised delivery timelines.

Non-Delivery & Returns

  • Undelivered Packages: If your order hasn’t arrived within the expected timeframe, contact support@naginawala.com within 30 days of purchase.
  • Damaged or Missing Items: Refuse delivery if the package is damaged or items are missing, and contact us immediately with details. See our Return Policy for more information.

Cancellation Policy

  • No Cancellations: Once an order is placed, cancellations are not permitted.
Need Help?
For any questions or concerns about shipping, tracking, or delivery, please reach out to our customer support at support@naginawala.com. We are here to make your shopping experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Happy shopping!
