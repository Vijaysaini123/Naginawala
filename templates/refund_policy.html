Naginawala Refund & Return Policy

At Naginawala, we are committed to quality and customer satisfaction. Please read our refund and return policy carefully before making a request.

Return & Exchange Guidelines

  • Returns or exchanges are accepted only if your order arrives damaged or you receive the wrong product.
  • Videographic proof of the product’s condition upon delivery is mandatory for any return or exchange request.
  • Requests must be made within 7 days of receiving your order, and items must be in original condition with all certifications and packaging intact.
  • No returns or exchanges for customized jewelry, bead bracelets, crystal trees, Rakhi, gift cards, or items received as ordered (unless damaged).
  • Personalized or custom-sized items are non-returnable.

Quality Assurance

If you doubt the authenticity of your gemstone, you may have it tested at a government-certified or internationally reputed gemological institute. If proven synthetic or artificial, you are entitled to a full refund or exchange.

All return shipping costs, duties, and taxes are the customer’s responsibility. We do not arrange pickups or issue credit notes for these charges.

Refund Process

  • Once your return is approved and received, we will inspect the item and notify you by email.
  • Approved refunds are credited to your original payment method within 10-12 working days (delays may occur due to bank processes or public holidays).
  • Refunds are only issued to the original payment source, in compliance with legal requirements.
  • For COD orders above ₹2 lakh, additional documents (e.g., PAN card, bank details) may be required for processing.
  • For prepaid orders, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 working days after approval.

Important Exclusions

  • No refunds or exchanges for change of mind.
  • No refunds for minor flaws or packaging issues that do not affect product quality.
  • No refunds for slight color or size variations due to screen or manufacturing differences.
  • All sale or discounted items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Order Cancellations

  • Orders cannot be cancelled once placed.

International Orders

  • Refunds (if approved) are processed within 40-45 days to the original payment method after receiving and inspecting the returned item.
  • Customers are responsible for all customs duties, VAT, and return shipping costs.
  • Refunds are only issued after the returned item is received and inspected.

Gifts

  • If your order was a gift shipped directly to you, you’ll receive a gift credit for approved returns.
  • If not, refunds are issued to the original purchaser.

Late or Missing Refunds

  • If you have not received your refund, check with your bank or card provider first.
  • If you still need help, contact us at support@naginawala.com.
Need Help?
For any questions or assistance regarding refunds or returns, please contact us at support@naginawala.com. We are here to help!
